# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 16, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Klaz ("we," "our," or "the Application"), developed by **Dhanesh U Pai** ("Service Provider"), collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application. 

By using Klaz, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

### 1. Information Collection and Use
To provide a personalized and gamified learning experience, we collect several types of information:

**A. Personally Identifiable Information (PII)**
While using our Application, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you, including:
* **Email Address:** Used for account authentication and security.
* **Display Name:** Used to personalize your dashboard and profile.
* **Profile Picture:** (Optional) If provided by you or via a third-party sign-in provider.

**B. Academic & Progress Data**
To tailor the syllabus and learning paths, we store:
* **Academic Details:** Your University, Branch, Semester, and Syllabus preference.
* **Learning Progress:** Quiz scores, module completion status, and time spent learning.
* **Gamification Stats:** Total EXP earned, current level, and 14-day challenge streak history.

**C. Automatically Collected Data**
We use third-party services (Firebase) that may collect information used to identify you and your device:
* Device IP address and unique Device IDs.
* Operating system version and device model.
* Application crash logs and performance diagnostics.

### 2. Third-Party Service Providers
The Application utilizes trusted third-party services to handle authentication, data storage, and analytics. These providers have their own privacy policies:

* **Google Play Services:** [Privacy Policy](https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/)
* **Firebase Authentication:** Used for secure user login.
* **Cloud Firestore:** Used to store your academic progress and stats.
* **Firebase Crashlytics & Analytics:** Used to monitor app stability and usage patterns. [Firebase Privacy Terms](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy/)

### 3. Artificial Intelligence (AI) Technologies
Klaz uses AI to enhance the learning experience. This includes:
* **Personalized Content:** Analyzing quiz performance to suggest areas for improvement.
* **Automated Summaries:** Generating insights based on your learning patterns.
All AI processing is performed securely within our infrastructure and adheres to the data protection standards outlined in this policy.

### 4. Data Retention and Deletion
* **Retention:** We retain your "User Provided Data" (Email, Progress, Stats) for as long as your account is active. Automatically collected technical data is typically retained for up to 24 months.
* **Deletion Rights:** You have the right to request the deletion of your data at any time. You can request account deletion by emailing us at **hello@klaz.app**. We will process your request and delete all associated PII and progress data within a reasonable timeframe.

### 5. Children’s Privacy
Klaz is a learning platform intended for students. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete this from our servers.

### 6. Security
The security of your data is important to us. We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit between the Application and Firebase. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

### 7. International Data Transfer
Your information, including Personal Data, is processed at our operating offices and in any other places where the parties involved in the processing are located (primarily Google Firebase server regions). Your consent to this Privacy Policy represents your agreement to that transfer.

### 8. GDPR & CCPA Compliance
* **For EU Users (GDPR):** You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data, and the right to data portability.
* **For California Users (CCPA):** You have the right to know what personal information is being collected and to request its deletion. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

### 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.
